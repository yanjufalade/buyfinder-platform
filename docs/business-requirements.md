# 1. Introduction

BuyFinder is an enterprise procurement platform that reverses the traditional marketplace model.

Instead of sellers advertising products, buyers publish purchase requests with specifications and a target price.

Suppliers compete by submitting offers to fulfil the request.

The buyer evaluates submissions and selects the most suitable supplier.

Depending on platform configuration, payment may either:

• be held securely in escrow until delivery confirmation

or

• be settled directly between buyer and supplier.

---

# 2. Business Objectives

The platform shall:

• Simplify procurement

• Increase supplier competition

• Improve pricing transparency

• Reduce procurement delays

• Provide secure financial workflows

• Maintain complete audit trails

• Support future AI-assisted procurement

# 5. Functional Requirements

The system shall allow buyers to:

• Register

• Verify email

• Login securely

• Create purchase requests

• Edit requests

• Cancel requests

• Review supplier offers

• Accept or reject offers

• Confirm delivery

---

The system shall allow suppliers to:

• Register

• Submit offers

• Upload product images

• Track offer status

• Withdraw offers

---

The system shall allow administrators to:

• Manage users

• Configure platform settings

• Enable or disable escrow

• Moderate listings

• View analytics

• Access audit logs
