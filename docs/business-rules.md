# Business Rules

## Purchase Requests

BR-001

A Purchase Request must belong to exactly one Buyer.

---

BR-002

A Buyer cannot modify the requested budget after the first supplier submits an offer.

---

BR-003

A cancelled Purchase Request cannot receive new offers.

---

BR-004

A Purchase Request automatically closes after its deadline.

---

## Offers

BR-005

Only registered suppliers with verified email addresses and verified mobile phone numbers may submit offers.

Higher trust levels unlock additional marketplace privileges, transaction limits, and buyer visibility.

---

BR-006

A supplier may submit only one active offer per Purchase Request.

---

BR-007

Offers may be edited until the submission deadline.

---

## Escrow

BR-008

If Escrow is enabled, payment must be successfully reserved before the Purchase Request becomes visible.

---

BR-009

Escrow funds remain locked until delivery confirmation.

---

BR-010

Only one payment release may occur for an escrow transaction.
